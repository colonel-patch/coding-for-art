Coding for Art

Lesson 11 - Final Project

In this lesson it is over to you to use what you have learned to create a piece of digital art.

Objectives

To use the techniques used int this course to generate either a humanoid figure or a car

The ptoject

The final construction should involve the following:

  • A series of object shapes which are linked together.
  • The work should involve a simple rotarey motion, such as a 'hand' waving, or the wheels on the car turning<
  • At least one taxture.
  • The work should respond to a keyboard or mouse event(s).
  • A position for the camera of the scene to best display the work.

Points awarded for meeting each requirement, and additional credit given for complexity and artustic merit.
